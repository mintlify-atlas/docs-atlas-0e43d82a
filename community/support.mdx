## When to Use Each Channel

### Discord & Matrix

Best for:
- Quick questions and real-time help
- Community discussions
- General troubleshooting
- Sharing experiences with other users

<Note>
The Discord server and Matrix space are bridged, so messages are synchronized between both platforms. Choose whichever platform you prefer!
</Note>

### Reddit

Best for:
- Longer-form discussions
- Sharing tips and tricks
- Community showcase
- General questions that might help others

### GitHub Issues

Best for:
- **Bug reports** - Found a reproducible issue? Create an issue!
- **Feature requests** - Have an idea for improvement?
- **Technical discussions** - Deep dives into specific problems

<Note>
Before creating a GitHub issue, please search existing issues to avoid duplicates. Include as much detail as possible: your OS, Prism Launcher version, and steps to reproduce the problem.
</Note>

## Tips for Getting Help

1. **Provide details** - Include your operating system, Prism Launcher version, and what you were trying to do
2. **Check logs** - Many issues can be diagnosed from log files
3. **Search first** - Your question might already be answered in our [FAQ](/community/faq) or documentation
4. **Be patient and respectful** - Our community members are volunteers helping in their free time
5. **Follow the Code of Conduct** - We maintain a welcoming, harassment-free environment for everyone
